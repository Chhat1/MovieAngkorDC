Dao was the only son of the commander Trakod. One day, Dao unexpectedly met Nang Nath and tried every possible way to win her as his partner. However, Nath was not interested because Dao was too flawed, which created a resentment in her. He harmed her without anyone noticing and forced her to become his servant. Yet, as they spent more time together, Neath began to fall in love with Dao instead. Dao’s father also fell ill and managed to arrange for them to become husband and wife, but it wasn't long before a group of bandits returned to kill Commander Trakod and the villagers.
